manav mangal school

This SMART LEARNER'S WORLD is located in Sector 11, Panchkula. The school enrols children of 3+ in to Nursery. The school is affiliated with the Central Board of  Secondary Education (CBSE), Delhi and  prepares students for the Board’s All India Secondary School  Examination.

School Facilities

General Facilities

This building is not just about concrete and steel. Every single part of this huge structure is alive to the needs of your child. The NOT-SO-COMMON FACILITIES offered here will not only answer to the needs of your child to your, entire satisfaction but will also make education highly exciting. 

  • Well-planned building (with elevator facility) on 3 acres
  • R.O Drinking Water Plant
  • Mobile-equipped Transport Facility
  • Uninterrupted Power Supply
  • Transport with Cameras & Vehicle Tracking System 
  • Parent School Online Alliance

Academic Facilities

  • 3 dimensional Smart Learning Lab
  • Each class multimedia ready with LCD TV/Interactive Touch Screen Board, Computer & Projector
  • Digital Labs : Maths Lab & English language Lab
  • Online access to Smart class learning content at home

Extra Curricular Facilities

  • State-of-the–art Auditorium 
  • Studios : Art & Craft, Dance & Music 
  • Health Care Program Under a whole time Lady Medical Officer
  • Kids Fantasy World
  • Skating Rink of International Standards
  • Tennis Courts of International Standards
  • Indoor Sports Complex

At manav mangal, we adopt a teaching methodology that is suited to the individual needs of learners and lesson plans are also based on multiple intelligence theories.
